Travel Information


From Hong Kong International Airport to CityU (Kowloon Tong MTR Station)

Located at Chek Lap Kok, Hong Kong International Airport is conveniently accessible via taxi, public buses or Mass Transit Railway (MTR).

Taxi Bus Route No. E22 MTR
Time approx. 30 minutes approx. 50 minutes approx. 45 minutes
Operating Hours / Frequency: 24 hours
  • From airport: 05:30 - 24:00
  • Frequency: around 15 minutes
  • From airport: 05:50 - 0:16
  • Frequency: 10 minutes
Fare: approximately HK$260 (US$34) including tolls, excluding levy for luggage and traffic HK$18 (US$2.3) HK$60 (US$7.8)
Where to take: Go to taxi stand according to the direction in the arrival hall.
  1. AIRPORT (GTC) BUS TERMINUS by following the signs from the departure area and walking along to the RIGHT. Once outside, you will see the bus stops.
  2. There is a Long Win Customer Service & Airbus Ticket Office where you can buy the bus ticket. *
  3. Get off at Shek Kip Mei Park, Cornwall Street. Head east along the Cornwall Street around 210 meters and then turn right onto the Tat Chee Avenue. Go along the Tat Chee Avenue about 280 meters to find the City University of Hong Kong Campus on the righthand side.
  1. After customs, walk straight through the Greeting Hall and board the Airport Express train on the same level.
  2. The single journey ticket can be bought at Airport Express Customer Service Centre or Ticket Issuing Machines. *
  3. Take the Airport Express train to Tsing Yi Station. Transfer to the Tung Chung Line at Tsing Yi station towards Hong Kong. Transfer to the Tsuen Wan Line at Lai King station towards Central. Transfer to the Kwun Tong Line at Prince Edward station towards Tiu Keng Leng. Get off the train at Kowloon Tong station and Exit at Exit C2.
  4. Go to the Level LG1 of Festival Walk shopping mall. Take the escalator between Shop LG1-10 & LG2-11. Pass through the pedestrian subway, and the main entrance of CityU is in front of you.(walk around 5 minutes).
* You may consider purchasing an Octopus Card from the Airport Train Station. They will charge HK$150 for this, 100 (value) and 50 (deposit). You can use this card on any local transport. When leaving Hong Kong, you can return the card at the Airport Train Station. Remaining value (including deposit) will be returned, save for HK$9 (handling fee).

From Local Hotels To Conference Venue

Take the MTR Line to Kowloon Tong Station and exit at Exit C2. Go to the Level LG1 of Festival Walk shopping mall. Take the escalator between Shop LG1-10 & LG2-11 to reach the Kowloon Tong entrance of City University of Hong Kong

Accommodation

Hyatt Regency Hong Kong, Sha Tin

It is located next to the Chinese University of Hong Kong and University MTR Station. It takes three stops to the Kowloon Tong Station where CityU is adjacent to

For more details, you can visit Hyatt Regency Hong Kong, Sha Tin.

Royal Plaza Hotel, Mong Kok

Royal Plaza Hotel is a luxury hotel situated in Mongkok in the heart of Kowloon. It takes only one stop to the Kowloon Tong Station.

For more details, you can visit Royal Plaza Hotel, Mongkok.

Harbour Plaza Metropolis, Hung Hom

It is situated in the heart of Kowloon overlooking the spectacular Victoria Harbour. Conveniently located across from Hunghom Station of MTR, it just takes two stops to the Kowloon Tong Station.

For more details, you can visit Harbour Plaza Metropolis, Hung Hom.

NTT International House, Kowloon Tong

The House is situated in the classy and quiet Kowloon Tong area, featured with the extraordinary view of Lion Rock Mountain and Hong Kong Island. It takes a mere 20 minutes walk from the House to CityU.

For more details, you can visit NTT International House, Kowloon Tong.
