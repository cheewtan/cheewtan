Dr. Tan received the M.A and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical Engineering from Princeton University. He is currently with College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University in Singapore. He was a postdoctoral scholar in the NetLab Group at Caltech, a senior fellow of the Institute for Pure and Applied Mathematics (IPAM) for the program Science at Extreme Scales, and was a visiting faculty at Tencent AI Lab and Qualcomm R&D (QRC). His research interests are distributed optimization, Generative AI, networks and edge learning. His Erdös number is 3.

Dr. Tan is serving or has served as the Editor for IEEE Transactions on Cognitive Communications and Networking, IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking and IEEE Transactions on Communications, BMC Archives of Public Health, IEEE Transactions on Computational Social Systems Special Issue on AI-Driven Technologies in Social Fintech and TPCs of IEEE INFOCOM, ACM SIGMETRICS, ACM MobiHoc, ACM Learning at Scale, IJCAI. He is also serving as the Publication Chair of the 2025 IEEE Conference on Artificial Intelligence (CAI) and the Symposium Co-Chair of 2025 IEEE Globecom Symposium on AI-Enabled Networks. He received an Honorable Mention award (Foundation Models and Generative AI Category) at the 2024 IEEE Conference on Artificial Intelligence, the Princeton University Wu Prize for Excellence, Google Faculty Award, several teaching awards including the Teaching Excellence Award at City University of Hong Kong, and was selected twice for the U.S. National Academy of Engineering China-America Frontiers of Engineering Symposium.
